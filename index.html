WHY KCCGO?

IDO Launchpad for Micro-Caps on Kucoin Community Chain

  • KCCGO is a deflationary NFT and Gaming tokens launchpad where 3% fee is charged on each transaction, where 33.3% is burned, 33.3% is distributed to the marketing wallet and 33.3% is redistributed among the holders.

  • KCCGO platform helps projects to reach out to potential investors. Investors who have met the tiers staking requirements are eligble to receive allocation in upcomming project.

  • KCCGO provides a platform for it's investors to stake KCCG tokens and gain access to invest in other token-sale on the launchpad.
